release - 04/05/2024

Okinawa (Complete Edition)

The complete edition of the Japanese-Infused lofi beats from the Okinawa series. With music from Okinawa and Okinawa II, plus three new tracks. Featuring Liam Gonen and S7VN.

release - 01/03/2024

Outside

The latest lofi single, following a few months after Okinawa II, with a new sound. A calming lofi track with sounds of the forest playing throughout.

release - 17/01/2024

Okinawa II

The second installment in the Japanese-Infused lofi sound of the Okinawa series. With three instrumentals and a brand new feature from Dunedin rapper, S7VN, over the "Cherry Blossoms" beat.

release - 06/01/2024

Stargazing

The first single from 2024. This track strays slightly from the typical lofi formula and into more of an rnb aesthetic. Although the lofi influences are still present in the drums.

release - 30/12/2023

Interlude

The last release of 2023. A slow, somber, reversed melody. This single was named "interlude" due to its vast shift in tone from the "Ruataniwha" album the month before.

release - 25/11/2023

Ruataniwha

The first full length album with 11 lofi tracks, including a bonus track created in 2020, years before the release of "sunsets". Named "Ruataniwha", after the lake near where the vast majority of the tracks were created.

release - 29/10/2023

Okinawa

This EP strays away from the familiar traditional lofi tracks of "Cloudy Skies" and "sunsets" into Japanese inspired melodies. This EP also has a guest feature from Dunedin artist, "Liam Gonen", on the track "Psychopath".

release - 26/10/2024

Cloudy Skies

The second lofi EP, following the succesful "sunsets" EP. "Cloudy Skies" follows up with six more chilled out lofi tracks, ending off with a jazzy, 80's style lofi outro.

release - 28/09/2023

sunsets

The first release from a new lofi producer. With six chillhop/lofi instrumental tracks, the "sunsets" EP is sure to be first of many lofi projects coming in the future.
